Who
we are

Maison Vale is a boutique digital marketing agency based in Cape Town, South Africa — built for brands that know what they stand for, and need the world to see it.

We work with a deliberate selection of fashion, beauty, lifestyle, and hospitality brands. The kind that has a point of view. The kind that deserve more than a content calendar and a boosted post.

Based in Cape Town. Operating globally. Small by design, sharp by nature.

Every brand we work with gets direct access — no account managers in between, no briefing chains. Just sharp thinking, applied fast.

What
we do

We start with a deep dive into your brand, then build a strategy around your reality — not a template. Retainers run monthly. No lock-in contracts. We earn your business every month.

Content Creation

Photo, short-form video, Reels, and TikTok content. We plan it, direct it, and deliver it — or work with what you have to make it land harder.

Strategy, content direction, scheduling, and community management for South African and international brands. Full-service or consultative.
